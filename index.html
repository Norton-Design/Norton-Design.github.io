About

Howdy! I'm Michael Norton, a software engineer based out of NYC. I come to software engineering from a background in the Arts where constant exploration and team projects forged me into the person that I am today. As I've walked down down the coding path, I've found that my artistic skills and values have become invaluable to the process: planning and exploring the problem before implementation, inspiring and fostering fellow team members in all pursuits, and the value of hard work in combination with creativity to make lasting solutions. These are my values as a partner.

If I'm not found jabbing away at a keyboard, I can be found training for climbing session at the local rock climbing gym or cycling around the city. I'm also an unabashed nerd that loves getting together with the gang for some tabletop gaming (DnD and Numenera, specifically) and jumps at the chance to play any game of Magic the Gathering I get my hands on!
